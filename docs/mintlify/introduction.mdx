## What is NextAPI?

NextAPI is a single, developer-first API for state-of-the-art video generation models. One key, one billing account, dozens of providers — no more juggling SDKs, quotas, or vendor contracts.

Think of it as **OpenRouter, but for Video AI**.

## Why NextAPI?

- **Unified endpoint** — text-to-video, image-to-video, and video-to-video across multiple providers with a consistent schema.
- **One of 20 official Seedance partners** — first-class access to ByteDance Seedance with priority capacity.
- **Transparent credits** — `1 credit = $0.001`. No subscriptions, no surprise invoices.
- **Async by default** — every generation is a job. Poll or use webhooks.
- **Production-ready** — HMAC-signed webhooks, sliding-window rate limits, idempotency, and detailed error codes.

## Who it's for

Builders shipping video features: creative tools, ad-tech, social apps, agents, and automation platforms that need reliable video generation without writing 10 integrations.
